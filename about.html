About Me

Meet the creator.

Hey there! I'm Nathan Jessop, a business student at Brigham Young University (BYU) with a deep-rooted passion for film. Ever since I was little, movies have been a big part of my life. I started delving into the world of cinema through Letterboxd reviews during high school, and now I run this movie review blog as a hobby to keep myself engaged and stay up to date on all things movie-related.

Film, to me, is more than just entertainment; it's a dynamic form of art that allows me to explore different perspectives, emotions, and stories. Whether it's the thrill of a suspenseful thriller, the epic journey of a superhero saga, or the rugged landscapes of a classic Western, each genre offers a unique experience that I find incredibly captivating.

Originally hailing from Nashville, Tennessee, I have a soft spot for southern cuisine, especially fried chicken. When I'm not buried in textbooks or lost in the world of cinema, you can find me enjoying a crispy piece of chicken while discussing my favorite movies with friends.
